Privacy notice

This notice explains what information Roza's Guest House receives when you search, enquire or make a direct booking, why it is needed and how you can contact us about it.

Who is responsible for your information?

Roza's Guest House, 9 Erekle Parjiani I Alley, Mestia 3200, Georgia, is responsible for personal information submitted through this website.

Information we collect

  • Your name, telephone number, email address and country.
  • Your stay dates, guest count, selected accommodation and preferences.
  • Notes you choose to provide, such as arrival or transfer information.
  • Booking identifiers, form timing and a temporary browser-session identifier used to protect the booking service from duplicate or automated submissions.
  • Messages you send through email or WhatsApp.

How we use it

We use this information to check availability, create and administer your reservation, contact you about your stay, provide requested assistance, prevent misuse of the booking service and meet applicable accounting or legal obligations. We do not sell guest information.

Services that process booking information

The public website is hosted using GitHub Pages. Direct-booking information is processed through Google Apps Script and may be stored in Google Sheets and sent through Gmail so the guesthouse can operate the reservation and send confirmations. WhatsApp and email are governed by their own providers' privacy terms when you choose those contact methods.

Retention and protection

Booking information is kept only for as long as reasonably necessary to manage the stay, handle enquiries or disputes, protect the booking service and satisfy applicable legal, tax or accounting requirements. Access to operational booking records is restricted to authorised guesthouse staff.

Your choices

You may ask what personal information the guesthouse holds about you, request correction of inaccurate details or ask for deletion where there is no legal or operational requirement to retain the information.
